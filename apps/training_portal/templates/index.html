Runs entirely in your browser over Web Serial against the ESP32-S3 plugged into your PC — no install, no AI-PC serial port. Flash the deploy benchmark firmware and your model first, then run this. Use Chrome or Edge. The AI PC only serves the dataset images.

Board photo storage (flash)

Two separate paths, one flash. The live preview above streams frames over USB CDC (Web Serial) and does not save anything. Photos you capture are stored on the ESP32-S3 flash — retrieve them either by downloading over CDC here, or by mounting the flash as a USB drive (MSC) on your PC. Capture saves a 96×96 model-input image (plus the full-resolution frame).

Click “List saved photos”. Download (CDC) pulls a photo over Web Serial; Mount as USB drive pauses preview and shows the files to your PC as a removable drive.

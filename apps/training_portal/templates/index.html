On-device benchmark (in your browser)

Runs entirely in your browser over Web Serial against the ESP32-S3 plugged into your PC — no install, no AI-PC serial port. Flash the deploy benchmark firmware and your model first, then run this. Use Chrome or Edge. The AI PC only serves the dataset images.

Flash the deploy benchmark firmware + a model, then click “Connect ESP32-S3 & run benchmark”.

Output-similarity vs a PC reference (Top-1/MAE/Cosine) is available from the developer CLI benchmark (see docs/LOCAL_LEGACY_README.md); the browser benchmark reports on-device accuracy, latency, and throughput.
